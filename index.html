Conference Project: History Of Maths across Continents

Abstract

     The history of mathematics is a rich tapestry that spans across time and cultures, reflecting the intellectual and scientific achievements of humanity. This abstract provides an overview of a comprehensive exploration of the history of mathematics across the world, highlighting the evolution of mathematical thought, its cross-cultural influences, and the profound impact it has had on various societies.he narrative begins in ancient Mesopotamia and Egypt, where the earliest recorded mathematical concepts emerged, including arithmetic, geometry, and algebra. It then delves into the world of classical Greece, showcasing the pioneering works of mathematicians like Euclid, Pythagoras, and Archimedes, whose insights laid the foundation for modern mathematical principles.
The narrative also unfolds in the Indian subcontinent, where ancient scholars developed concepts such as the numeral system and the concept of zero, which revolutionized mathematics and led to breakthroughs in astronomy and calculus.
